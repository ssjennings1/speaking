About Dr. J

The higher you climb, the fewer people tell you the truth.

Your team watches what you react to. Your board wants confidence. Your peers are competitors. There's no one left who will tell you the thing you actually need to hear — or ask the question that cracks the problem open.

That's the gap I work in. I bring the analytical tools of a strategist and the human insight of a clinician — 20 years inside leadership systems, trained in the psychology of organizations and the architecture of how they actually behave under pressure. I see what the dynamics in your business are doing to your results. And I know how to work with people through the hard parts, not just around them.

Sometimes that's a single session to get clarity on a decision that's been sitting too long. Sometimes it's a year of calls that become the one place you can think out loud without it costing you anything.
